Best London Restaurants for Tiramisu

CANTO CORVINO

A chocolate orb dusted with gold power. Coffee amaretto jelly bursts out of Savoyardi sponge, soaked in coffee syrup and marscarpone marsala cream. Crunch comes from milk chocolate feuilletine on top.

TRULLO

A generous portion of marscapone with delicious chocolate dust on top. It’s so popular that it will be on the menu at Trullo’s second restaurant, Padella, opening in March near Borough Market.

POLPO

A small pot, densely packed with creamy custard and espresso-soaked lady fingers, covered in cacao, with a shot of rum. Yum.
